About

I am a PhD student in computer graphics and computer vision, currently studying at the University of Rey Juan Carlos, under the supervision of Professor Dan Casas Guix. My research consists in solving well-known problems in hand tracking and 3d reconstruction for computer vision and VR/AR/XR.
