About

PhD in Computer Vision & Graphics, I enjoy building systems, solving complex problems, and turning ideas into concrete results. Passionate about computer vision and 3D data, I’ve contributed to deep learning pipelines, geometry processing workflows, and real-time graphics tools. Curious, autonomous, and persistent, I’m driven by meaningful challenges and technical depth, and eager to contribute to impactful projects in computer vision or 3D data processing.
